## Task
Generate diverse WildChat-style prompts paired with oracle probe questions for activation oracle training.

## Key Requirements
- User prompts (wildchat_question) + oracle questions
- ~50% questions should have NEGATIVE answers (probe wrong topics/emotions)
- Mix of binary (yes/no) and open-ended questions
- Diverse topics and question types

- Cost tracking included
